Terminology and FAQ

  1. Hodler - a person who stays in the club until the target price is reached. They can be a "Mature Hodler" or an "Immature Hodler."
  2. Founder - the person who created the contract (also a Hodler).
  3. Coward - a person who leaves the club before the price is reached (leaving behind some percentage of coins).
  4. Baddie - a person who tries to game the system by joining the club just before the target price is reached to get the coins left by cowards.
  5. Mature Hodler - a Hodler who has been marked or has marked themselves mature, which can only be done after a certain number of blocks. (This is to Prevent baddies).
  6. Immature Hodler - a Hodler who is not been marked mature.
  7. Admin the admin gets a small percentage of coins left by cowards.

About the Lighthouse:

The price is monitored by a Lighthouse contract. A Lighthouse is a type of oracle. The Lighthouse contract has the price of in USD pushed to it periodically by third party volunteers who run the Lighthouse's Java application.

In this way anyone can query the Lighthouse to see if the current prices is greater or equal to the target price (this only has to happen once). The Lighthouse MUST be queried from the hodlers club contract when the current price greater than or equal to the target price..

The club can also be disbanded if 50% of the members vote to disband. A reason for disbanding is because the Lighthouse is no longer reliable. Only those marked Mature can vote. If the club is disbanded there is no penalty or reward for leaving the club.
